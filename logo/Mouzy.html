The Mouzy’s Story

The Mouzy’s Story

Our journey began in 1985, under the group name "Shimla Juice Shop", which our beloved father initiated. Back then, we were a single shop in the trade market of Perinthalmanna. Our father's primary goal in that venture was to deliver quality drinks with hygiene at an affordable price. In 2001, we introduced Avil milk in the Shimla Juice Shop. It received a lot of acceptance from food lovers. Since then, through numerous trials and errors, we have shaped a unique brand for a single drink, Avil milk. It was the birth of MOUZY on March 2020, in Perinthalmanna.

What's Inside?

Our Avil milk is actually a concoction of mashed bananas, roasted aval or rice flakes, milk, and peanuts for an additional crunch. Roasted Avil adds to the uniqueness of the dish, lending a crunchy texture to the drink. Each ingredient added to the drink is full of vital nutrients required for the body.
